TIC TAC TOE GAME

OBJECTIVE:
This project aims to develop a Tic Tac Toe game using Java.
It mainly consists of developing and implementing a desktop application for playing Tic Tac Toe in three different modes, each mode will be clearly explained in the below sections.

GAME RULES AND DESCRIPTION:
Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game. In this game, there is a board with 3 x 3 squares. The two players take turns putting their own symbols on the 3x3 board.
To win in Tic Tac Toe game you need to be one of the players to get three same symbols in a row either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on the 3 x 3 grid.
The player who first gets 3 of his/her symbols in a row - vertically, horizontally, or diagonally wins the game, and the other loses the game.
The game can be played by three modes which are:
(1) Single Mode:
In this mode you choose to play against the computer, and you are allowed to choose the level of the difficulty before the game begins.
(2) Two-player Mode:
In this mode you choose to play against another player who is using your device, the second player is asked to submit his name and then the game begins with the same mentioned rules.
(3) Online Mode:
In this mode you choose to play online against another player who is online at the same time as you and exists on your network. you have two options 
either you choose one of the online players and send an invitation to invite him/her to play with you and a dialogue appears to the other player asking if he/she wants to accept 
your invitation, or you can stay online and wait for other online player to send you an invitation and you are asked if you want to accept this incoming invitation.
  
SET-UP FOR THE GAME:
First, you double click on the provided Jar file to open the game. After the game opens you need to connect to the server before you start playing in any mode. 
Then, you should have an account to login using this account, if not, you are asked to register using a unique username and a password.
